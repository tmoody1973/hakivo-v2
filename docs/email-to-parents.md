## What is Hakivo?

You know how it's really hard to keep up with what's happening in Congress and your state legislature? There are thousands of bills being introduced every year, and unless you have hours to spare reading dense legal documents, most people have no idea what their elected officials are actually doing.

**Hakivo solves this problem.**

It's a website and platform that uses artificial intelligence to:

1. **Track legislation that matters to YOU** - Users pick topics they care about (healthcare, environment, education, etc.) and Hakivo automatically finds and follows relevant bills

2. **Explain bills in plain English** - Instead of reading 50-page legal documents, users get clear summaries like "This bill would lower prescription drug prices by allowing Medicare to negotiate directly with pharmaceutical companies"

3. **Create personalized audio briefings** - Every morning, users can listen to a 5-10 minute podcast-style update about what's happening with the issues they care about, just like having a personal news anchor

4. **Find your representatives** - Type in your address and instantly see your U.S. Senators, Representative, and state legislators with their photos, contact info, and how they've been voting

5. **AI Assistant** - Users can ask questions like "What bills are being proposed about student loans?" and get real-time answers with sources

## How Does It Work?

Behind the scenes, Hakivo:

- **Pulls data from official government sources** (Congress.gov for federal bills, OpenStates for state legislation)
- **Uses AI to analyze and summarize** complex legislation (powered by Claude, the same AI I'm talking to you through)
- **Generates human-quality audio** using Google's text-to-speech technology
- **Creates a daily podcast** called "100 Laws That Shaped America" that automatically publishes to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, etc.
- **Syncs everything automatically** - new bills are added daily, news is refreshed multiple times per day

The whole system runs on 61 different services working together in the cloud, handling everything from payment processing (for the paid subscription tier) to generating professional PDF reports.

I believe everyone should be able to understand what their government is doing without needing a law degree or hours of free time. Democracy works better when citizens are informed.
