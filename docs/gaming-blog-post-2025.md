## Content Summary

### News Tower
- **Developer**: Sparrow Night
- **Publisher**: Twin Sails Interactive
- **Release**: November 18, 2025 (Full release after 2 years Early Access)
- **Price**: $19.99 (currently 20% off)
- **Platform**: Windows, macOS via Steam
- **Rating**: Overwhelmingly Positive (95% from 2,400+ reviews)
- **Steam Link**: https://store.steampowered.com/app/1649950/News_Tower/

**Key Features**:
- 1930s newspaper management in Depression-era New York
- Navigate between Mayor, Mafia, Military, and High Society
- Editorial decision-making (respect vs reach)
- Manage 30+ job roles
- District-based strategy from Harlem to Wall Street

### Political Arena
- **Developer/Publisher**: Wayside Press
- **Release**: September 2, 2025 (Early Access)
- **Price**: $14.99
- **Platform**: Windows, macOS via Steam
- **Rating**: Mostly Positive (75% approval)
- **Steam Link**: https://store.steampowered.com/app/1670920/Political_Arena/

**Key Features**:
- Congressional political simulation
- Create custom politicians with ideology/skills/agenda
- Draft bills and secure votes through negotiation
- Dynamic NPC relationships and alliances
- Staff management system
- Influence White House priorities
