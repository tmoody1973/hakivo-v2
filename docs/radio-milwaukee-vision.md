## Part I: Radio Milwaukee's Unique Position

### Music + Stories: A Distinctive Model

Radio Milwaukee is not a typical public radio station. Its model combines:

**Music Discovery**
- Curated playlists featuring independent and local artists
- "Discover Milwaukee Music" as a core mission
- Genre-diverse programming that defies format restrictions
- First-play opportunities for emerging artists

**Community Storytelling**
- Local news coverage with Milwaukee focus
- Artist profiles and behind-the-scenes stories
- Community event coverage and promotion
- Neighborhood spotlights and cultural reporting

**Community Engagement**
- Music-based community building
- Events and live performances
- Local business partnerships
- Educational initiatives

This combination creates opportunities for AI augmentation that pure news or pure music stations cannot match.

### The Personalization Opportunity

Current radioâ€”even great radio like 88Nineâ€”delivers the same experience to every listener. But listeners have different:

| Dimension | Variation |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Music taste** | Genre preferences, discovery appetite, familiarity needs |
| **Location** | Neighborhood, commute patterns, venue proximity |
| **Time** | Morning energy, evening wind-down, weekend exploration |
| **Interests** | Local news, artist stories, event info, pure music |
| **Depth** | Quick hits vs. deep dives, casual vs. superfan |

AI enables Radio Milwaukee to serve each listener's unique combinationâ€”without losing the curatorial voice that makes 88Nine distinctive.

## Appendix A: Feature Specifications

### Personalized Playlist Generation

**Inputs**:
- User listening history (88Nine streams)
- Explicit preferences (genres, artists)
- Time of day / day of week
- Location / neighborhood
- 88Nine staff picks and playlists
- Local artist database

**Algorithm**:
1. Build user taste profile from listening + preferences
2. Score all 88Nine-curated tracks against profile
3. Apply local artist boost (2-3x weight for Milwaukee artists)
4. Apply discovery quotient (balance familiar/new)
5. Filter through 88Nine quality threshold
6. Generate playlist with explanation for each track

**Output**:
- Personalized playlist (refreshed daily)
- "Why this song" explanations
- Local artist highlights
- DJ/staff pick callouts

### Audio Briefing Generation

**Content Sources**:
- 88Nine news stories (human-written)
- Milwaukee event calendars
- User's recommended music (from playlist engine)
- Weather APIs
- Artist news relevant to user's taste

**Generation Process**:
1. Select stories relevant to user location/interests
2. Identify music connections for each story
3. Generate briefing script using templates
4. Synthesize audio with 88Nine voice style
5. Insert music previews at appropriate points
6. Add personalized music recommendations

**Quality Control**:
- Human review of script templates
- Audio quality verification
- Fact-check against source material
- User feedback integration

### Community Matching

**Connection Types**:
- Concert companions (similar taste + geography)
- Genre communities (deep interest groups)
- Neighborhood music fans (hyper-local)
- Artist supporters (fan communities)

**Matching Algorithm**:
- Taste similarity score
- Geographic proximity
- Activity patterns (event attendance)
- Explicit preferences
- Safety/moderation filters

**Privacy Safeguards**:
- Opt-in only
- No direct contact without mutual consent
- Moderated introductions
- Report/block functionality
- Anonymous until confirmed
