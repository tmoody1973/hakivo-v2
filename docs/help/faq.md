# Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Hakivo.

## General

### What is Hakivo?
Hakivo is a civic engagement platform that transforms Congressional legislation into personalized audio briefings and provides AI-powered tools to help you understand and engage with the legislative process.

### Who is Hakivo for?
- Citizens who want to stay informed about legislation
- Advocates tracking specific policy areas
- Students learning about civics
- Journalists covering Congress
- Anyone curious about how laws are made

### Is Hakivo nonpartisan?
Yes. Hakivo provides factual information about legislation and representatives without political bias. We present voting records and bill summaries objectively.

### Where does Hakivo get its data?
- **Congress.gov**: Official source for federal legislation
- **OpenSecrets**: Campaign finance data
- **GovTrack**: Historical voting records
- **State legislature APIs**: State-level bills
- **News APIs**: Current events and coverage

## Account & Privacy

### How do I create an account?
Visit hakivo.com and click Sign Up. You can register with email/password or use Google Sign-In.

### Is my data private?
Yes. We don't sell your personal data. Your tracked bills, policy interests, and chat history are private. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### Can I delete my account?
Yes. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.

### What happens to my data if I cancel Pro?
Your data is preserved when downgrading. You keep access to existing documents and tracked items (within Free limits).

## Daily Briefings

### How are briefings personalized?
Briefings are generated based on:
- Your selected policy interests
- Your location (state/district)
- Bills you're tracking
- Representatives you follow

### When are briefings available?
New briefings generate each morning. Set your preferred time in Settings > Policy Interests.

### Why is my briefing short?
Briefing length varies based on Congressional activity. Slow legislative periods produce shorter briefings.

### Can I get briefings via email?
Pro subscribers can enable daily email briefings in Settings > Notifications.

## Congressional Assistant

### What can I ask the Assistant?
- Bill information and summaries
- Representative voting records
- How legislation works
- Policy area questions
- Document generation

### Is the Assistant always accurate?
The Assistant uses official Congressional data and AI analysis. While highly accurate, always verify critical information with primary sources.

### Are my conversations private?
Yes. Your conversations are encrypted and not shared with third parties.

### Can I share conversations?
Yes, use the Share Thread feature to create a shareable link.

## Bills & Legislation

### How current is bill information?
Bill data syncs with Congress.gov multiple times daily. Most updates appear within hours.

### What's the difference between federal and state bills?
- **Federal**: U.S. Congress legislation (available to all users)
- **State**: State legislature bills (Pro feature)

### Why can't I find a specific bill?
- Check the bill number format (H.R. vs HR)
- The bill might be from a previous Congress
- Try searching by title or sponsor

### What do bill statuses mean?
| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Introduced | Filed but not yet considered |
| In Committee | Under committee review |
| Passed House/Senate | Approved by one chamber |
| Signed | Enacted into law |
| Vetoed | Rejected by President |

## Representatives

### How do you determine my representatives?
Your ZIP code maps to your Congressional district. We then identify your district's House representative and your state's two Senators.

### What if my ZIP code crosses district lines?
Try entering your full address in the search, or contact support for manual assignment.

### Can I view any representative, not just mine?
Yes! Use the Representatives search to find and view any member of Congress.

## Technical Issues

### The audio won't play
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure your browser allows audio
- Try a different browser
- Clear your browser cache

### I'm not receiving emails
- Check your spam folder
- Add support@hakivo.com to contacts
- Verify notifications are enabled in Settings

### The site is slow
- Check your internet connection
- Try clearing browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Contact support if issues persist

### Bill/representative data seems wrong
- Data updates several times daily
- Very recent changes may take a few hours
- Report persistent errors to support
