# Settings & Preferences

Customize your Hakivo experience through the Settings page. Access it by clicking your profile icon and selecting **Settings**.

## Profile

Your profile information determines your personalized experience.

### Personal Information
| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| First Name | Displayed in greetings and documents |
| Last Name | Used for account identification |
| Email | Login and notifications |

### Location
| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| ZIP Code | Identifies your Congressional district and representatives |
| State | Enables state legislation tracking |
| City | Optional, for local context |

**Note**: Your Congressional district is automatically determined from your ZIP code.

### Updating Your Profile
1. Edit any field
2. Click **Save Changes**
3. Changes take effect immediately

## Policy Interests

Your policy interests drive personalized briefings and content recommendations.

### Available Topics
Select from 12 policy areas:
- Environment & Energy
- Health & Social Welfare
- Economy & Finance
- Education & Science
- Civil Rights & Law
- Commerce & Labor
- Government & Politics
- Foreign Policy & Defense
- Housing & Urban Development
- Agriculture & Food
- Sports, Arts & Culture
- Immigration & Indigenous Issues

### Best Practices
- **Select 3-5 interests** for focused, relevant briefings
- **Too many interests** may dilute personalization
- **Review periodically** as your priorities change

### Brief Delivery Time
Choose when your daily briefing is generated:
- 6:00 AM, 7:00 AM, 8:00 AM, 9:00 AM, or 10:00 AM (your local time)

## Notifications

Control how Hakivo communicates with you.

### Email Notifications
| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Email Notifications | Master toggle for all emails |
| Bill Updates | Alerts when tracked bills have activity |
| Weekly Summary | Digest of the week's activity |

### Notification Frequency
- **Immediate**: Get alerts as events happen (Pro only)
- **Daily Digest**: One email per day with all updates
- **Weekly Summary**: Consolidated weekly email

## Audio

Customize your listening experience for briefings and podcasts.

### Playback Speed
Choose your preferred speed:
- **0.75x**: Slower, for careful listening
- **1.0x**: Normal speed
- **1.25x**: Slightly faster
- **1.5x**: Popular for regular listeners
- **1.75x**: Fast consumption
- **2.0x**: Maximum speed

### Additional Options
| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Autoplay Next | Automatically play the next briefing when one ends |
| Skip Intro/Outro | Jump straight to the main content |

## Tracked Items

Manage all your tracked legislation and followed representatives in one place.

### Federal Bills
View all tracked federal bills with:
- Bill number and title
- Current status
- Latest action
- Untrack button

### State Bills (Pro)
View tracked state legislation with:
- Bill number and title
- State and chamber
- Status
- Untrack button

### Summary
See your total tracked items count at a glance.

## Documents

Manage reports and presentations generated by the Congressional Assistant.

### Viewing Documents
All generated documents appear here, showing:
- Document title
- Type (Report, Slides, Analysis)
- Creation date
- View count (if shared)

### Document Actions
| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Download PDF | Save as PDF file |
| Download PPTX | Save as PowerPoint (slides only) |
| Share | Generate a public shareable link |
| Make Private | Disable public sharing |
| Delete | Permanently remove the document |

### Filtering
Use the filter dropdown to show:
- All Documents
- Reports Only
- Slides Only

## Subscription

Manage your plan and view usage.

### Current Plan
See whether you're on:
- **Free**: Basic access with limits
- **Hakivo Pro**: Full unlimited access

### Usage Statistics
Track your usage with progress bars:
| Resource | Free Limit | Pro |
|----------|------------|-----|
| Audio Briefs | 3/month | Unlimited |
| Tracked Bills | 3 | Unlimited |
| Followed Members | 3 | Unlimited |
| Documents | 3/month | Unlimited |

### Premium Features Status
Check marks indicate which Pro features you have access to.

### Managing Subscription
- **Upgrade**: Click "Upgrade to Pro" to subscribe
- **Manage**: Pro users can update payment or cancel

## Account

Security and account management options.

### Password
Click **Change Password** to update your password:
1. Enter current password
2. Enter new password
3. Confirm new password
4. Click Save

### Connected Accounts
View and manage OAuth connections:
- Google account status
- Disconnect option

### Danger Zone
**Delete Account**: Permanently delete your account and all data.
- This action cannot be undone
- All tracked items, documents, and history will be deleted
- You'll need to confirm before deletion

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I change my email?**
Contact support to change your account email address.

**Will changing my ZIP code affect my tracked bills?**
No, but it will change which representatives appear as "yours."

**Can I export my data?**
Contact support for a data export request.

**How do I cancel Pro?**
Go to Settings > Subscription > Manage Subscription.
