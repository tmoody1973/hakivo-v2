# Subscription & Pricing

Hakivo offers a free tier for casual users and a Pro subscription for power users who want unlimited access.

## Plans Overview

### Free Tier - $0/month
Perfect for trying Hakivo or occasional use.

**Includes:**
- 3 audio briefings per month
- Track up to 3 federal bills
- Follow 3 representatives
- Basic bill summaries
- Full access to "Signed Into Law" podcast (all 100 episodes)
- Congressional Assistant (10 questions/day)
- Email support

### Hakivo Pro - $12/month or $99/year
For engaged citizens who want to stay fully informed.

**Everything in Free, plus:**
- Unlimited audio briefings
- Unlimited bill tracking
- Follow unlimited representatives
- Daily personalized email briefings
- Deep AI analysis of legislation
- Real-time vote alerts
- State legislature tracking
- Unlimited Congressional Assistant access
- Generate unlimited reports and presentations
- Export documents as PDF and PowerPoint
- Priority support

### Teams & Organizations - Custom Pricing
For advocacy groups, newsrooms, and government affairs teams.

**Includes:**
- Everything in Pro
- Team member management
- Shared tracked legislation
- Custom briefing topics
- API access
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom integrations

Contact sales@hakivo.com for pricing.

## Subscribing to Pro

### Monthly Subscription
1. Go to **Settings > Subscription** or the **Pricing** page
2. Click **Subscribe to Pro**
3. Enter payment information
4. Start using Pro features immediately

### Annual Subscription
Save 31% by paying annually:
- Monthly: $12/month = $144/year
- Annual: $99/year (equivalent to $8.25/month)

Select "Annual" during checkout.

## Payment Methods

We accept:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay

All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

## Managing Your Subscription

### Viewing Subscription Status
Go to **Settings > Subscription** to see:
- Current plan
- Renewal date
- Payment method on file
- Usage statistics

### Updating Payment
1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Update payment method in the customer portal

### Canceling
1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Select **Cancel Subscription**
4. Your Pro access continues until the end of your billing period

## Billing

### When You're Charged
- Monthly: Same day each month (based on signup date)
- Annual: Same day each year

### Receipts
Receipts are emailed automatically after each payment. Access past receipts in the customer portal.

### Failed Payments
If payment fails:
1. You'll receive an email notification
2. We'll retry the payment
3. Update your payment method to avoid service interruption

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is there a free trial?
The Free tier lets you try all core features. You can upgrade to Pro anytime.

### Can I switch from monthly to annual?
Yes! Contact support or use the customer portal. You'll receive prorated credit for unused months.

### What happens when I cancel?
You keep Pro access until your current billing period ends. Then you revert to the Free tier. Your data (tracked bills, documents) is preserved.

### Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer full refunds within 14 days of purchase. Contact support@hakivo.com.

### Are there discounts for nonprofits or educators?
Yes! We offer 50% off Pro for:
- Verified 501(c)(3) nonprofits
- K-12 teachers and professors
- Students with valid .edu email

Contact support@hakivo.com with verification.

### Can I pause my subscription?
Not currently, but you can cancel and resubscribe later. Your data is retained.

### Is my payment information secure?
Yes. We use Stripe for payment processing and never store your full card details on our servers.

## Upgrading Mid-Cycle

When you upgrade from Free to Pro:
- Pro features activate immediately
- Your first billing cycle starts today
- No prorated charges for the free period

## Downgrading

If you exceed Free tier limits after canceling Pro:
- Tracked bills over 3: Oldest tracked bills are preserved but you can't add new ones
- Documents: All documents remain accessible but you can't generate new ones
- Briefings: You'll have 3 new briefings available next month

## Contact

For billing questions or issues:
- Email: support@hakivo.com
- Use the in-app feedback widget
- Response time: Within 24 hours (Priority for Pro subscribers)
