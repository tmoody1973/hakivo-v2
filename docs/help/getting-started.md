# Getting Started with Hakivo

Welcome to Hakivo! This guide will help you get set up and make the most of your civic engagement platform.

## What is Hakivo?

Hakivo transforms Congressional legislation into personalized audio briefings and interactive civic engagement tools. Think of it as your personal legislative aide, helping you stay informed about the laws and policies that matter most to you.

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit [hakivo.com](https://hakivo.com) and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your email address and create a password, or sign up with Google
3. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
4. Complete your profile setup

## Setting Up Your Profile

Your profile helps Hakivo personalize your experience. Here's what to configure:

### Location Information
- **ZIP Code**: Enter your 5-digit ZIP code to automatically identify your Congressional district
- **State**: Select your state for state-level legislation tracking
- **City**: Optional, helps with local representative information

Your location determines which representatives appear on your dashboard and which state bills are relevant to you.

### Policy Interests
Select the topics you care about most. These interests power your personalized daily briefings:

- Environment & Energy
- Health & Social Welfare
- Economy & Finance
- Education & Science
- Civil Rights & Law
- Commerce & Labor
- Government & Politics
- Foreign Policy & Defense
- Housing & Urban Development
- Agriculture & Food
- Sports, Arts & Culture
- Immigration & Indigenous Issues

You can select multiple interests, and we recommend choosing 3-5 for the most focused briefings.

## Your Dashboard

After setup, your dashboard becomes your civic engagement hub:

- **Your Representatives**: Quick access to your Senators and House Representative
- **Daily Brief**: Your latest personalized audio briefing
- **Podcast**: Latest episode from "Signed Into Law"
- **Latest Actions**: Recent votes and actions from Congress
- **Personalized Content**: News and updates matching your interests
