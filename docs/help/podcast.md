# Signed Into Law Podcast

"Signed Into Law" is an AI-generated educational podcast series covering the 100 most consequential U.S. laws from 1900-2000. Each episode explores the history, impact, and legacy of landmark legislation.

## About the Series

### What It Covers
The podcast explores transformative laws including:
- The Social Security Act
- Civil Rights Act of 1964
- Clean Air Act
- Americans with Disabilities Act
- And 96 more landmark laws

### Episode Format
Each episode includes:
- Historical context of the era
- The problem the law addressed
- Key figures and debates
- How the bill became law
- Impact and legacy today
- What we can learn

### Episode Length
Episodes range from 10-25 minutes, designed for:
- Commute listening
- Lunch breaks
- Casual learning
